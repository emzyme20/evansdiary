## Post-Op

I was returned to the ward at 7am. I don't remember much this day, I was asleep most of the day and I can't really remember Emma and Evan being there just about all day.

Usually the first few hours post-op are groggy but patients slowly recover during the day and are sometimes sitting up or even moving around by the end of the day. Emma says she knew I wasn't getting better and was really worried about me when she went home. She called my mother for support and that in turn brought forward my mum's visit from Sunday to Saturday (the next day). Emma had obviously said something that had really scared my mother too.
