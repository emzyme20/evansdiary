*"I bring myrrh"* was the line that Evan had to say in his nursery nativity play this year. Evan didn't falter when he appeared on the stage in his homemade King outfit. I wasn't sure when asked to supply the outfit whether he would wear it because he usually reacts funnily to odd looking clothes, but he really embraced this nativity play. He wanted to wear his King's crown every day.   

Evan will be moving up to the Butterfly room at nursery when we return from the Christmas holiday period. He has been having half day visits leading up to the end of term to help him transition easily. He will be reunited with his old play buddies Jos and Bradley. We'll miss his favourite member of the nursery staff Lucy who will remain in the Caterpillar room that he is moving up from.
